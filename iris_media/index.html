ABOUT IRIS MEDIA COMMUNICATIONS

IRIS MEDIA COMMUNICATIONS is a group of wonderful broadcasting teams and other media services. Our major commodity is information, and we aim to help disseminate concrete and worthwhile information to students on campus. While we may not reach a large number of audiences, we strive to reach all students.
